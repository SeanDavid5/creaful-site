Mission

A silent tradeoff is reshaping modern health.

GLP-1 drugs are transforming weight loss — but they come with an overlooked cost: up to 40% of weight lost is muscle, not fat.

The Problem

More than 50 million Americans are using or considering GLP-1 medications. At the same time, women in midlife are losing muscle at twice the rate of men.

Muscle isn’t just aesthetic — it’s metabolic health, longevity, and physical independence. Yet there is no purpose-built solution designed to preserve it.

Why Now

The fastest-growing drug category in history is colliding with one of the most underserved problems in health: muscle preservation.

Doctors already recommend creatine. Patients are already searching for answers. The demand exists — but the solution hasn’t caught up.

Our Insight

Muscle preservation isn’t solved by stacking ingredients. It requires delivering both the signal and the energy for muscle to survive.
